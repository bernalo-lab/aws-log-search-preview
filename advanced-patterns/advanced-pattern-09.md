# Advanced Pattern 09 - Distinguish Responsibility from Blame

**Advanced Pattern 09 - Distinguish Responsibility from Blame**

**When to use:**  
When defensive behaviour or silence appears during an incident.

---

## The Pattern

Assume human performance degrades over time — just like infrastructure.

Adjust behaviour accordingly:

- Shorter investigative loops
- More explicit handovers
- Fewer assumptions

---

## Why This Works

Make it explicit that **responsibility ≠ blame**.

Responsibility:

- Enables action
- Clarifies decision-making

Blame:

- Shuts down information
- Slows resolution

---

## Common Mistake

Avoiding ownership entirely to prevent blame.

---

## How This Connects to Recipes

- Encourages honest logging and reporting
- Improves signal quality in **Recipe 25** (near misses)
- Makes post-incident reviews more accurate

---

## Operational Insight

Blame suppresses the very information incidents need.
