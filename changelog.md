# Changelog

A version history documenting updates, improvements, and newly added recipes to the AWS Log Search Recipes handbook.

---

## Changelog

### v1.0

- Initial release
- 35 log search recipes covering failure, degradation, and ambiguity
- Advanced Patterns for incident reasoning and decision-making
- Practical guidance on using the handbook during live incidents
- Glossary of common terms and concepts used throughout

### v1.1

- Added 5 new recipes related to recovery and failover scenarios
- Clarified Advanced Pattern 04 with additional examples
